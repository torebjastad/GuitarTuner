# Piano Health & Tuning Marketplace - Product Requirements Document

## Core Concept
An integrated platform consisting of a mobile app for piano owners and a web portal for professional piano tuners. The platform uses audio analysis to diagnose piano tuning health and connects owners with local tuners for fixed-price assignments.

## Target Audience
- **Piano Owners:** Individuals who want to monitor their instrument's health and find reliable tuning services.
- **Piano Tuners:** Professionals looking for new clients and pre-qualified leads with diagnostic data.

## Key Features & User Flows

### 1. Piano Owner App (Mobile)
- **Guided Recording:** User is prompted to play specific keys (e.g., all white keys). The app visualizes the audio capture.
- **Tuning Curve Analysis:** Generates a visual graph (tuning curve) showing the deviation of each recorded note from the ideal pitch.
- **Health Recommendation:** An automated assessment (e.g., "Good," "Needs Attention," "Critical") based on the analysis.
- **Service Request:** Users can post an assignment with a fixed price. The diagnostic report and audio are attached automatically.
- **Marketplace Interaction:** View tuner profiles, accept assignments, and coordinate schedules.

### 2. Tuner Web Portal (Desktop)
- **Assignment Feed:** A list of available jobs in the tuner's area with fixed pricing and distance.
- **Diagnostic Review:** Before accepting, tuners can listen to the user's recording and analyze the tuning curve to assess the work required.
- **Job Management:** Track accepted assignments, communicate with owners, and mark jobs as complete.

## Monetization Model
- **Commission-Based:** The platform takes a percentage cut from every completed tuning assignment.
- **Fixed Pricing:** Assignments are posted with a pre-approved price to simplify the transaction for both parties.

## Visual Direction
- **Professional & Precise:** High-contrast graphs, clean typography, and a "technical yet accessible" feel.
- **Trustworthy:** A color palette that evokes craftsmanship (e.g., deep blues, warm wood tones, or ivory/ebony accents).
- **Functional:** Clear calls to action and intuitive navigation for both owners and busy professionals.
